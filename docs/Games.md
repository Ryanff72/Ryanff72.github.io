# Games

I have held an interest in game design for a long time, and in 2019 I finally decided it was time to learn how to make games! Since then, I have gained experience working in Blender, Unreal Engine, Unity, and Visual Studio! I enjoy working on projects with others, and have learned a lot through making games. I have worked developers, programmers, and artists from around the world through [game jam](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Game_jam) competitions. I also enjoy working with members of my school's programming club on projects.
